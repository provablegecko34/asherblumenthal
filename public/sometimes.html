Sometimes

Sometimes, the world feels quiet, like a Sunday morning before anyone else wakes...

On those days, I wake to a familiar feeling—stillness, an unspoken pause...

Sometimes, I wonder if everyone feels this way. Not lost, exactly, but wandering...

Like that afternoon in summer when I stood barefoot in the rain, the sky torn open, laughing...

And sometimes, I still hear those whispers...

Sometimes, the answers aren’t clear, and that’s okay...

On those days, I find myself standing at the edge of something bigger than I can understand...

But on those days, I move anyway...

And so, on those days, I hold onto that...

On those days, I remember to listen...
